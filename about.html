Our story

Crafting Quality Since 2008

To provide Balch Springs and surrounding areas with exceptional carpentry that combines traditional craftsmanship with modern techniques, ensuring every project exceeds expectations.

Juan Carpenter Inc was founded in 2008 by Juan Martinez, a master carpenter with a passion for woodworking. Starting with small repair jobs, Juan built a reputation for reliability and precision that quickly spread through Balch Springs. Over the years, the company grew from a one-man operation to a full-service carpentry team, taking on complex custom builds and renovations.

Today, we continue Juan's legacy of quality. Our team of skilled craftsmen shares a commitment to excellence, using time-honored techniques and the finest materials. We treat every home like our own, ensuring that each project reflects our dedication to beauty, function, and durability.
