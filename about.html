Our story

Craftsmanship You Can Trust

To provide Balch Springs with exceptional carpentry that enhances homes and businesses through quality materials, skilled workmanship, and outstanding customer service.

Juan Carpenter Inc was founded with a simple philosophy: every piece of wood deserves respect and every project deserves excellence. Starting as a one-man operation, Juan built a reputation for precise cuts, solid joints, and beautiful finishes. Over the years, the business grew by word of mouth as satisfied customers recommended his work to neighbors and friends.

Today, we serve the entire Balch Springs area with a dedicated team of skilled carpenters. We take pride in our work, whether it's a custom kitchen cabinet, a backyard deck, or a simple repair. Our commitment to quality and integrity remains the foundation of everything we do.
